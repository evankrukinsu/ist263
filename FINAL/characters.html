Meet the Characters!


DAENERYS TARGARYEN

Daenerys Targaryen, also known as Dany and the Mother of Dragons, is the last known family member of the ancient Targaryen Dynasty. She begins her journey as a timid girl sold into marriage, but over time, she transforms into a powerful queen with a loyal following. Her determination to reclaim the Iron Throne leads her across the Narrow Sea to Westeros. Daenerys’ journey is marked by her compassion for the oppressed and her desire to create a just world. However, her unwavering belief in her destiny also leads to moments of ruthlessness.


JON SNOW

Jon Snow, initially perceived as Ned Stark’s illegitimate son, embarks on a transformative journey at the Wall, serving the Night’s Watch. His leadership skills and unyielding sense of duty see him rise to become the Lord Commander. His life takes another dramatic turn when he is declared the King in the North, affirming his pivotal role in the realm’s power dynamics.


CERSEI LANNISTER

Cersei Lannister, a central figure from House Lannister, is the queen of King’s Landing. Her journey is marked by her endless pursuit of power and her unwavering and strong dedication to her family. Cersei’s sly strategies and political maneuvering often keep her adversaries on their toes. Despite the challenges and betrayals she faces, Cersei remains unyielding, using every setback as a stepping stone towards securing her power. Her complex character arc, marked by moments of both vulnerability and ruthlessness, makes her one of the most compelling figures in Game of Thrones.


SANSA STARK

Sansa Stark, the eldest Stark daughter, and Jon’s younger sister, evolves from a naive young girl into a strong political participant. She endures numerous hardships but uses these experiences to become a key leader in the North.


MELISANDRE

Melisandre, also known as The Red Woman, is a priestess in the religion of R’hllor, who possesses prophetic visions and magical abilities. Her interpretation of these visions significantly influences the series’ events.


MARGAERY TYRELL

Margaery Tyrell of House Tyrell becomes queen through her marriages to Renly Baratheon, Joffrey Baratheon (Lannister), and Tommen Baratheon (Lannister). She is known for her intelligence and political skills. She would do anything for her family and house, especially her brother Loras and grandmother.


TYRION LANNISTER

Tyrion Lannister, also known as “the Imp”, is known for his wit and intellect. He is often mistreated because of his dwarfism, but despite that, he becomes one of the most powerful political players in Westeros.


ARYA STARK

Arya Stark is the younger daughter of House Stark with a fierce spirit. She rejects traditional female roles to pursue her path as a warrior and assassin, throughout the whole world of Game of Thrones.

BRAN STARK

Bran Stark possesses mystical powers that allow him to see into the past and future. After becoming the Three-Eyed Raven, he plays a crucial role in the battle against the White Walkers.
